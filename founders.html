Founders

We Woman Have Our Own Backs

We are an all-female law public safety team who believe that women are overlooked in the public safety field. We started C.R.O.W.N. to prove that women can do anything that a man can do and look good while doing it. It is run by four female highly trained professional women who had a dream to show what we are capable. C.R.O.W.N. started in 2020 and is ran by Yahaira Lopez, Mackenzie Evinger, Kimberly Cordova, and Michelle Fuentes.
